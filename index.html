I am a corporate and commercial lawyer with over 7 years of experience, trained in a common law jurisdiction and fully accredited in Canada through the National Committee on Accreditation (NCA). Having passed all NCA examinations, I am an active LSO candidate based in Toronto, bringing internationally tested legal expertise and a deep understanding of complex commercial environments directly into the Ontario market.

My experience spans contract lifecycle management, commercial advisory, and corporate compliance across some of the most demanding legal environments in the profession. At EY, I led end-to-end CLM for 30+ agreements — MSAs, SOWs, and Change Orders — while serving as the primary legal point of contact for three internal US client teams. At Kochhar & Co., one of India's premier law firms, I negotiated 200+ complex commercial agreements for a leading international airline across multiple jurisdictions.

Few lawyers bring the combination I do: deep transactional experience, cross-jurisdictional exposure, a management track record from a Big 4 firm, and the credentials of a published legal author. I have authored four books under LexisNexis, with forewords by senior managing partners of leading law firms — a reflection of the depth of legal thinking I bring to every role and every client relationship.

Whether you are a law firm looking to strengthen your contracts practice or a corporation seeking sharp commercial instincts and meticulous drafting skills, I am ready to contribute from day one.
